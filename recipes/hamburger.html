Chicken Burger

Description

This is a delicious burger where the main ingredient is the chicken.

The burger has a nice bread, tomato, lettuse, and sauce

Ingredients

For the chicken patties:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated onion
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano or preferred herbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For assembling the Burger

  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Sliced tomatoes
  • Sliced onions
  • Cheese slices
  • Condiments

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken patties
  2. Cook the chicken patties
  3. Assemble the burgers
  4. Add the chicken patties
  5. Add Cheese
  6. Add Condiments
  7. Put it all together
  8. Serve
